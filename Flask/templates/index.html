Our Story

What is YogaLens?

✦

Yoga is a 5000-year-old practice developed in ancient India by the Indus-Sarasvati civilization. The word yoga means deep association and union of mind with the body. It keeps both mind and body in equilibrium through asana, meditation, and several other techniques.

Nowadays, yoga has gained worldwide attention due to increased stress levels in the modern lifestyle. While yoga can be practised in centres or with personal tutors, many people prefer self-learning — but in self-learning, incorrect posture can be harmful to health, resulting in acute pain and long-term chronic concerns.

YogaLens bridges that gap. Upload a photo and our Xception-based model instantly identifies your pose, giving you the confidence to practise safely on your own.
